Everything you need to know about ASKFLEETS delivery services.

Simply create an account, enter your pickup and drop-off locations, choose your delivery type (Standard, Express, Freight, or Scheduled), and confirm your booking. You'll receive a tracking link instantly via SMS and email.

ASKFLEETS operates across all major cities and inter-city corridors. We support point-to-point deliveries — from a single parcel to full freight loads — across our partner network including UPS, FedEx, DHL, USPS, and Uber Freight.

Every shipment gets a unique tracking ID. Use the "Track Consignment" button on our homepage or enter your ID at any time. Live GPS updates are provided every 30 seconds, and you'll receive proactive SMS alerts at each delivery milestone.

Our 24/7 support team is always available. In the event of a delay, you'll be notified immediately with a revised ETA. For lost shipments, we initiate a trace within 2 hours and provide full compensation as per our delivery guarantee policy.

We handle documents, small parcels, medium and large packages, palletised freight, perishables (cold chain), and scheduled bulk loads. Hazardous materials require prior approval — contact our support team for clearance before booking.
